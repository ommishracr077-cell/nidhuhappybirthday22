## How to use
1. Unzip the folder.
2. Replace the placeholder images inside `/photos` with your own 4 pictures. Keep the filenames:
   - `photo1.jpg`, `photo2.jpg`, `photo3.jpg`, `photo4.jpg`
3. Open `index.html` in Chrome (desktop or phone). Everything runs offline.

## Customization tips
- Edit lines in `index.html` to change texts easily (search for each section by id).
- You can tweak colors in `assets/style.css` under the `:root` variables.
- Add more sweet messages in `assets/script.js` (`sweetMsgs` array) for the mini-game.
- To add more pages, extend the `PAGES` array in `assets/script.js` and duplicate a section in `index.html`.
